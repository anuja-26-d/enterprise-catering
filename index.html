Welcome to Our Website

DELICIOUS FOOD, EXCELLENT SERVICE

DRT Enterprises is a complete food program solution for Corporates. We are bringing the cafeteria into the 21st century with a digital food platform that offers companies and foodservice vendors a transparent, easy-to-use cafeteria experience that increases management efficiency while delivering meals to employees with convenience and ease.


DRT Enterprises solves different pain points related to unorganized vendors, inefficient workflows, and a complete lack of technology in this space. Some of the problems we encounter and mitigate with our service are: long lines, no support for a hybrid workforce, challenges in quality assurance and lost revenue from cash handling.


DRT Enterprises offers vendor management where top notch vendors can be swapped and rotated to maintain consistent great taste.DRT believes in the spirit of continuous enhancement and hence we keep adding new features and technologies to automate and digitize corporate cafeterias.
